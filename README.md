# Title
**Student Voice - A Decentralized Feedback & Accountability Platform**

# Description
In university environments, students often hesitate to share honest feedback, report issues, or suggest improvements due to concerns about privacy and potential repercussions. I developed **Student Voice** to bridge this gap. Built on the Stellar blockchain, this platform ensures that every student's voice is heard anonymously and recorded permanently. Because the data is stored on a decentralized ledger, no authority can delete or alter the feedback, ensuring a truly transparent and democratic campus life.

# Features
* **Anonymous Student Authentication:** Students use a unique hash identifier to post, allowing the system to verify they belong to the university without revealing their personal name or ID.
* **Immutable Records:** Once a suggestion or complaint is submitted, it is written to the Stellar blockchain. It becomes a permanent record that cannot be hidden or modified.
* **Community-Driven Upvoting:** A transparent voting mechanism where students can support the most pressing issues. Each verified student has only one vote per post to prevent manipulation.
* **Smart Escalation:** When a post reaches a specific threshold (e.g., 5 votes), the contract automatically updates its status to "Escalated," signaling that the university administration needs to take formal action.
